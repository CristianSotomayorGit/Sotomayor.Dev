Archivy.io

Jan 2022 - Present

Archivy.io is a technology incubator and developer. Our mission is to catalyze the advent of automation in architecture and construction by;

• Researching and testing the feasibility of new methods and workflows;

• Working with architects, engineers and designers to plan and implement design automation strategies;

• Increasing efficiency and freeing professionals from unnecesary tasks;

• Enabling users to chanel creative thought and energy onto valuable work.

It is our vision at Archivy that we will soon live in a future when the built environment will be healthier, affordable and more beautiful. To achieve this, Archivy and the rest of the construction industry must advocate for the use of friendly materials, the reduction of wasteful practices, and enabling the creatives to imagine our world without constrains.
